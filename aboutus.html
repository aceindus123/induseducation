About Us

Indus Group of companies is established in 2001 in the name of Indus-USA Inc in Farmington Hills, Michigan, USA. Indus means industries in French and as the company is based out of USA so, the name is derived from Industries of USA. It is one of the fastest growing multinational companies in the areas of engineering and information technology solutions. In the last 8 years, Indus has serviced more than 25 customers in Automotive, Aerospace, Energy and Oil & Gas sectors.

Indus is known for excellent customer care and always has received 100% customer satisfaction from all the customers serviced so far, the fact that 90% of the new customers have been through referrals supports our claim.

Indus always provided the low cost solutions to the global market through associated companies and partners. Indus-USA Inc has started its own branch Indus Integrated Technology Solutions Pvt Ltd in Bangalore, India in the year 2005.

Indus having been in the global market for the last 8 years and servicing various industries, we recognize the need for the skilled resource pool who are abreast with the latest technology and tools. To fulfill the need of the industry and the qualified individuals Indus has launched INDUS GLOBAL ACADEMY OF TECHNICAL EDUCATION (I-GATE) in the year 2008.
